Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 28, 2026

TryFreed ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

1. Information We Collect

Account information

  • Email address (used for login and account recovery)
  • Password (stored as a secure hash — we never see your plain-text password)

Financial data you enter

  • Income and expense records you create manually inside the app
  • Savings goals and debt tracking entries
  • Budget categories and notes

We do not collect bank credentials, credit card numbers, Social Security numbers, or any data from financial institutions. All financial data in TryFreed is entered manually by you.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and operate the TryFreed app
  • To sync your data across your devices
  • To process your subscription payment via Stripe
  • To send you transactional emails (e.g., password reset, billing receipts)
  • To improve the product based on aggregated, anonymized usage patterns

We never sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for advertising or marketing purposes.

3. Third-Party Services

We use a limited set of trusted third-party services to operate TryFreed:

  • Supabase — Cloud database and authentication. Your financial data is stored in Supabase's secure infrastructure. Supabase Privacy Policy
  • Stripe — Payment processing. Stripe handles all billing data; we never store your credit card information. Stripe Privacy Policy
  • GitHub Pages — Hosting for the TryFreed landing page. GitHub Privacy Statement

These providers are contractually bound to protect your data and may not use it for their own purposes beyond what is necessary to provide the service.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored in Supabase, which uses industry-standard encryption at rest and in transit (TLS 1.2+). We apply row-level security so that only your authenticated account can access your records.

5. Your Rights

  • Access: You can view all your data inside the TryFreed app at any time.
  • Export: You may request a full export of your data by emailing us at support@tryfreed.com.
  • Deletion: You may delete your account and all associated data at any time from the app settings. Data is permanently deleted within 30 days of the request.
  • Correction: You can edit or delete any financial entry directly within the app.

6. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you cancel your subscription, your data remains accessible until you choose to delete your account. Deleted accounts are purged within 30 days.

7. Children's Privacy

TryFreed is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by email or via an in-app notice. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision.

9. AI Assistant — External Data Processing / Assistente IA — Processamento Externo

EN — What happens when you use the AI Assistant

When you send images (receipt photos, screenshots), audio recordings, or spreadsheets to the AI Assistant, the following applies:

  • Images are converted to base64 format on your device before transmission.
  • All data is sent over HTTPS (TLS) — never in plain text.
  • The content is processed by the Claude API (Anthropic Inc.) to extract financial information.
  • Images and audio are not stored on our servers. They are discarded immediately after the AI response is returned to your device.
  • Excel and CSV files are parsed entirely in your browser using SheetJS; only the extracted text content is sent to the AI.
  • Conversation history is stored locally in your app session only — not on TryFreed servers.
  • Anthropic may process this data in accordance with their own privacy policy: anthropic.com/legal/privacy.

We do not use your images, audio, or spreadsheet content for training purposes. All AI interactions are ephemeral.

Consent: By clicking the ✨ AI Assistant button, you acknowledge that your input may be sent to Anthropic for processing. You may decline at any time simply by not using the AI Assistant feature.

PT — O que acontece quando você usa o Assistente IA

Quando você envia imagens (fotos de boletos, prints), gravações de áudio ou planilhas para o Assistente IA, o seguinte se aplica:

  • As imagens são convertidas para base64 no seu dispositivo antes de serem transmitidas.
  • Todos os dados são enviados via HTTPS (TLS) — nunca em texto puro.
  • O conteúdo é processado pela API Claude (Anthropic Inc.) para extrair informações financeiras.
  • Imagens e áudio não são armazenados nos nossos servidores. São descartados imediatamente após a resposta da IA ser retornada ao seu dispositivo.
  • Arquivos Excel e CSV são analisados inteiramente no seu navegador usando SheetJS; apenas o conteúdo de texto extraído é enviado para a IA.
  • O histórico de conversa é armazenado localmente na sua sessão do app — não nos servidores do TryFreed.
  • A Anthropic pode processar esses dados conforme sua própria política de privacidade: anthropic.com/legal/privacy.

Não utilizamos suas imagens, áudio ou planilhas para fins de treinamento. Todas as interações com a IA são efêmeras.

Consentimento: Ao clicar no botão ✨ Assistente IA, você reconhece que seus dados podem ser enviados à Anthropic para processamento. Você pode recusar a qualquer momento simplesmente não usando o recurso Assistente IA.

10. Your Rights — LGPD / GDPR

EN — Your data rights

  • Right of Access: You may request a copy of all personal data we hold about you by emailing support@tryfreed.com.
  • Right to Erasure: You may delete your account and all associated data at any time from app Settings → ··· → Delete Account. Data is permanently purged within 30 days.
  • Right to Rectification: You may correct or update any financial entry directly within the app at any time.
  • Right to Portability: You may export your data in Excel format from Dashboard → Export at any time.
  • Right to Object / Restrict Processing: Contact us to request restriction of processing or to object to any specific use of your data.

For formal LGPD or GDPR requests, email support@tryfreed.com with subject "Data Rights Request" and a copy of a valid identity document. We will respond within 30 days.

PT — Seus direitos sobre os dados

  • Direito de Acesso: Você pode solicitar uma cópia de todos os dados pessoais que mantemos sobre você enviando e-mail para support@tryfreed.com.
  • Direito de Exclusão: Você pode deletar sua conta e todos os dados associados a qualquer momento em Configurações → ··· → Deletar Conta. Os dados são excluídos permanentemente em até 30 dias.
  • Direito de Retificação: Você pode corrigir ou atualizar qualquer registro financeiro diretamente no app a qualquer momento.
  • Direito de Portabilidade: Você pode exportar seus dados em formato Excel em Dashboard → Exportar a qualquer momento.
  • Direito de Oposição / Restrição: Entre em contato para solicitar restrição de processamento ou para se opor a qualquer uso específico dos seus dados.

Para pedidos formais de LGPD ou GDPR, envie e-mail para support@tryfreed.com com o assunto "Pedido de Direitos sobre Dados" e cópia de documento de identidade válido. Responderemos em até 30 dias.
