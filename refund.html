30-Day Money-Back Guarantee

If you're not happy with TryFreed for any reason within the first 30 days of your subscription, contact us and we'll issue a full refund — no questions asked.

Eligibility

You are eligible for a full refund if:

  • You request the refund within 30 days of your initial subscription purchase (monthly or annual)
  • This is your first subscription — the guarantee applies once per account

Refunds are not available for:

  • Renewal charges after the first billing period
  • Requests made more than 30 days after the charge
  • Accounts found to be in violation of our Terms of Service

How to Request a Refund

1
Send us an email

Email support@tryfreed.com with the subject line "Refund Request".

2
Include your account email

Let us know the email address associated with your TryFreed account so we can locate your subscription.

3
We process your refund

We'll confirm and process your refund within 3 business days. The amount will appear back on your original payment method within 5–10 business days, depending on your bank or card issuer.

Cancellation vs. Refund

Cancelling your subscription and requesting a refund are two separate actions:

  • Cancellation stops future billing. You retain access to TryFreed until the end of your current billing period. It does not automatically trigger a refund for the current period.
  • Refund returns the payment you already made. If you want your money back within 30 days, you must request it explicitly by emailing us.

You can cancel anytime from your account settings under Subscription → Cancel plan.

AI Assistant & Included Features

The AI Assistant (voice input, image processing, and Excel import) is included in your TryFreed subscription at no extra charge. Use of these features does not constitute a separate purchase and does not affect your eligibility for the 30-day money-back guarantee.

O Assistente IA (voz, imagem e importação de Excel) está incluído na sua assinatura do TryFreed sem custo adicional. O uso desses recursos não constitui uma compra separada e não afeta sua elegibilidade para a garantia de 30 dias.

Partial Refunds

We do not offer partial or prorated refunds for unused portions of a billing period outside of the 30-day guarantee window. If you have an exceptional circumstance, reach out and we'll do our best to help.
